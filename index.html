Human Activity Recognition Using Random Forest


Build a Random Forest model to classify human activities into 6 main classes namely - laying, sitting, standing, walk, walk-up and walk-down. The designed random forest classifier achieves an accuracy of 94% on the test data.

School Budget Prediction


Budgets for schools and school districts are huge, complex, and unwieldy. Built a multi-class-multi-label classifier to classify and predict where and how schools are using their resources.

Global Food Production Analysis


Finding solutions for feeding the growing world population has become a hot topic for food and agriculture organizations. This project aims to explore the total production (food and feed) year-wise, item-wise and country-wise and to know the fastest growing countries in terms of food production & consumption.

Predicting Hypoglycemia Using Linear Regressor


Implemented a liner regression model to predict the glucose level and thereby the occurrence of Hypoglycemia using the CGM time series glucose data.

Crime Detection In Smart Cities using IoT


Used Apache's PySpark to process the textual features of the San Francisco's Crime dataset. Built a simple logistic regression model to classify the crime data into 36 distinct categories thus making it a multi-classification problem achieving 97% accuracy.

Explore and Summarize Prosper Loan Data


Prosper is a peer-to-peer lending platform that aims to connect people who need money with those who have money to invest. In this project, my aim was to explore and analyze the Prosper loan data spanning 81 variables.

Prediction of Arrhythmia Using IoT and AI


Arrhythmia is a group of conditions where the heart rate is irregular i.e either too fast (above 100 beats per minute) or too slow (below 60 beats per minute). A support vector classifier is trained on the dataset (from UCI Machine Learning Repository) and achieves an accuracy of 78% on this binary classification problem.

Fake News Classifier


Two classifiers namely a Count-Vectorizer (using bag of words concept) and TfIDf-Vectorizer are built to classify and determine fake from real news. On using MultinomialNB, it was found that Count-Vectorizer visibly outperformed the TF-IDF vectors.

Sentiment Analysis of Amazon Products


In this project, I analyze sentiments of baby products from Amazon and find which is the popularly purchased product, by building a sentiment classifier.
